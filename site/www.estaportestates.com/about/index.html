    ABOUT ESTAPORT

    A Legacy of Trust. A Story Built Over Generations.

    Estaport is a family-owned legacy real estate company in Lagos, with roots that span over five decades and a formal corporate journey that began in 1971.

    "For the Nigerian diaspora, Estaport represents a bridge to home—providing secure, legally verified property investments with the same discipline that has sustained us for generations."

    OUR BEGINNINGS

    Founded on Vision & Integrity

    Founded by the Osomo family, Estaport emerged at a time when real estate development in Lagos demanded foresight, courage, and integrity. From its early years, Estaport adopted a structured approachaligning land, infrastructure, and financing so its projects could support long-term, mortgage-backed homeownership.

    The Estaport story is one of vision, discipline, and long-term value creation built by people who understood Lagos before it became the global megacity it is today.By 1979, Estaport was executing landmark developments, establishing itself as a trusted private developer working alongside major public institutions.
